About Fire Prevention Month

March is observed as Fire Prevention Month in many countries. It is a time when people are encouraged to learn about fire safety and take measures to prevent fires from occurring.

Fire Prevention Tips

Here are some tips to help you prevent fires:

  • Keep flammable materials away from heat sources.
  • Install smoke detectors in every room.
  • Keep a fire extinguisher handy and learn how to use it.
  • Develop a fire escape plan and practice it with your family.
